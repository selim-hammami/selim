Mesut Özil :

Mesut Özil was born on October 15, 1988.
He is a professional German footballer who plays as an attacking midfielder for the İstanbul Başakşehir. He is known for his technical skills, vision, creativity, and passing skills.
Özil started his football career in 1995 and has brought many trophies to Arsenal.
